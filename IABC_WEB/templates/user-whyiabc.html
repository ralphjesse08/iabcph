Why Join IABC?

Whether you're an in-house communicator, independent consultant, academic or a student, IABC has all the relevant resources, connections and learning opportunities you need to move your career forward.


Make a bigger impact in your job


Tap into hundreds of case studies, articles, webinars and other resources for new ideas, fresh insights and assistance in day-to-day problem-solving.

Learning to stay sharp


Keep growing with discounts on professional development through IABC Academy online courses, the IABC World Conference, the IABC Gold Quill Awards, and with chapter and regional events in your local area.



Connections to help you advance


Find mentors and tap into a global network of peers for answers to pressing questions through The Hub, a private online community for IABC members only.



Content to keep informed


Stay current with Catalyst magazine articles and other content on critical industry topics delivered to your inbox each week.



Tools to leverage for results


Access critical information when you need it, from an online library that includes award-winning case studies, webinars, podcasts and more.

Credentials to raise your profile


Get discounts on professional certification through the Global Communication Certification Council (GCCC).
